Boeing Flight Services at EAA AirVenture 2017

If you are planning on attending AirVenture in Oshkosh, Wisconsin this year, come visit Boeing at exhibit #474 from Monday, July 24 to Sunday, July 31. The display offers a glimpse into the future of training; highlights include:

  • 787 Flight Demonstrator
  • Photo booth featuring F16, Chinook, MAX 1st flight, Red Barn, 787 flight deck, space launch
  • Boeing Learning Solutions — eAIRCRAFT
  • The future of training — HoloLens
  • Commercial and military models

Current or retired commercial pilot, technician, cabin crew, or airline employee, can sign in at the Boeing-sponsored commercial crew registration tent next to the Boeing exhibit, and connect with friends in the industry.

Visitors can also participate in the VFR Pilot Sectional Scavenger Hunt where Boeing pilots will guide the kids through the activity using a paper map and worksheet. Upon completion, the pilots will show them the navigational charts with Jeppesen’s Mobile FliteDeck on iPads.

The Other Boeing companies will also be present, including Jeppesen, Aviall, and Inventory Locator Service (ILS).

We look forward to seeing you in Oshkosh in July!
